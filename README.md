# Business Statistics Portfolio 📊🚀

Welcome! This repository documents my analytical journey through the Master’s in Business Analytics at UNIE Universidad. Here, I translate academic mathematical foundations, statistical inference, and machine learning models into **clear, risk-adjusted strategic recommendations** that any business owner can use to grow revenue and optimize operations.

Instead of hiding behind academic jargon, this portfolio focuses entirely on **Business Outcomes**:
*   How to spot hidden market patterns.
*   How to run scientific experiments (A/B testing) to maximize marketing spend.
*   How to deploy predictive models to capture customer value before your competitors do.

---

## 🗺️ Master Portfolio Roadmap

The data and workflows in this repository are organized into four strategic pillars, moving from raw data diagnostics to automated predictive machine learning pipelines:

### 1. Data Foundations, Data Quality & Human Bias 🧠
*   **The Business Problem:** Companies waste millions of euros building models on dirty, unvetted data or making operational choices driven by team cognitive shortcuts[cite: 4].
*   **Core Concepts:** The DIKW Pyramid (Data $\rightarrow$ Information $\rightarrow$ Knowledge $\rightarrow$ Wisdom)[cite: 10], identifying data anomalies[cite: 9], and neutralizing executive decision biases (Anchoring, Confirmation, and Availability biases)[cite: 3].
*   **Production Assets:** 
    *   `Ejercicio1_Tema1.docx`: High-throughput operational bottleneck analysis (University Coffee Shop Case)[cite: 6].
    *   `Ejercicio2_Tema1.docx`: Digital product unit margin efficiency evaluation[cite: 5].
    *   `Ejercicio1_DIKW_Tema2.xlsx`: Structural aggregation of raw social media feedback data.

### 2. Descriptive Analytics & Executive Data Storytelling 📈
*   **The Business Problem:** How do you translate thousands of rows of customer records into an engaging narrative that guides executive investment without creating analysis paralysis[cite: 4, 9]?
*   **Core Concepts:** Measures of central tendency (Mean, Median, Mode)[cite: 8], data dispersion metrics (Standard Deviation, Outlier Isolation)[cite: 8, 9], marketing funnel conversion optimization, and Media Mix Modeling (MMM).
*   **Production Assets:**
    *   `Ejercicio1_Tema3.docx` & `Ejercicio2_Tema3.xlsx`: Conversion funnel audits and social media sentiment storytelling[cite: 7].
    *   `advertising_excel.xlsx` & `escenarios_prediccion.xlsx`: Historical multi-channel ad spend models and forward-looking allocation budgets.
    *   `Ejemplo__tiendas.csv` & `ANalysis multivariente tiendas.ows`: Multi-channel business performance profiling.

### 3. Experimental Design & Statistical Inference (A/B Testing) 🔬
*   **The Business Problem:** If a business introduces a new price or promotion and sales go up, how do they prove the campaign *caused* the increase rather than a random fluke?
*   **Core Concepts:** Hypothesis Testing, Confidence Intervals, and Analysis of Variance (ANOVA) to validate commercial significance across multiple parallel testing groups.
*   **Production Assets:**
    *   `Datos_Banca_ANOVA.omv` / `.xlsx`: Financial promotional incentive validation lab (Cashback vs. Zero Commissions).
    *   `Datos_Estrategias_Precios.omv` / `.xlsx`: E-Commerce markdown elasticity and baseline pricing significance testing.

### 4. Multivariate Descriptives & Predictive Classifiers 🤖
*   **The Business Problem:** Modern customer profiles contain too many moving variables for traditional filters. Businesses need machine learning to dynamically cluster users and forecast behaviors[cite: 2].
*   **Core Concepts:** Dimensionality Reduction via Principal Component Analysis (PCA), Unsupervised Clustering, K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN), and Naive Bayes probabilistic mapping[cite: 1].
*   **Production Assets:**
    *   `Ejemplo1_PCA_Clientes.omv` / `.xlsx`: Compressing multi-dimensional customer data into core loyalty components.
    *   `Aanalysis multivariente bancos. Clustering.ows`: Machine learning demographic profile mapping.
    *   `kNN Bank clients Orange PM.ows` & `Naive Bayes Bank Clients Orange PM.ows`: Automated supervised pipelines predicting individual client choices.

By combining rigorous statistics with modern analytics tools, this portfolio proves the capacity to take a raw, unstructured corporate dataset and extract clear answers to three vital business questions:
1.  **What happened, and why?** (Descriptive Analytics & Outlier Identification)[cite: 8, 9]
2.  **Which strategic choice is mathematically guaranteed to perform best?** (Statistical Inference)
3.  **What will our customers do next week?** (Predictive Machine Learning Classifier)[cite: 1]
