# 📈 Theme 2: Descriptive Analytics, Funnels & Media Mix Strategy

## 🏢 Executive Overview
Descriptive analytics answers a vital question: *What exactly is happening across our marketing funnels right now?* This module focuses on distilling massive consumer datasets into narratives that optimize budget allocations and maximize the return on ad spend (ROAS).

---

## 🔍 Deep-Dive Project Breakdowns

### 1. Data Storytelling: Turning Customer Pain Points into Profit
*   **The Academic Source:** `Ejercicio1_Tema3.docx` / `Ejercicio2_Tema3.xlsx`[cite: 5]
*   **The Business Problem:** Marketing channels are flooded with customer mentions, but the team cannot identify where the most severe brand risk lies[cite: 5].
*   **The Core Analytics Insight:**
    *   By cleaning up tracking logs, we cross-reference total brand mentions against negative sentiment vectors[cite: 5]. 
    *   While "Delivery" generates 670 mentions, its negative rate is restricted to 32%[cite: 5]. Meanwhile, **"Price"** flags 620 mentions but suffers an aggressive **57% negativity score**[cite: 5].
*   **The Strategic Decision:** The primary friction point isn't operational logistics—it is price perception[cite: 5]. We pause downstream operational overhauls and prioritize introducing tiered subscription models or immediate loyalty pricing structures to stabilize customer sentiment[cite: 5].

### 2. Media Mix Modeling: Forward-Looking Revenue Prototyping
*   **The Academic Source:** `advertising_excel.xlsx` / `escenarios_prediccion.xlsx` / `Tarea 2 Prediccion.ows`
*   **The Business Problem:** A business owner needs to divide marketing investments across TV, Radio, and Newspaper channels without guessing where the next euro of sales comes from.
*   **The Solution Engine:** 
    *   Running multivariate descriptive profiles (`Orange Tiendas Column Statistics.ows`) to discover core historical relationships between media channels and gross sales.
    *   Building multi-variable tracking models to evaluate how specific changes in channel budgets affect target performance goals.
*   **The Strategic Decision:** Deploying the forward-looking dashboard (`escenarios_prediccion.xlsx`) to simulate investment combinations. The model highlights severe margin decay when over-indexing on print newspapers and unlocks hidden scale benefits within optimized digital/radio combinations.

### 3. Market Research: Cross-Sectional Consumer Survey Profiling
*   **The Academic Source:** `Encuesta habitos.xlsx` / `.omv` / `Encuesta habitos medidas.pdf` / `Ejemplo__tiendas.csv`
*   **The Business Problem:** Launching products without identifying the foundational behaviors and distribution profiles of the core target demographic.
*   **The Solution Engine:** Analyzing thousands of rows of university student data using central location metrics (Mean, Median) and dispersion bounds (Standard Deviation) to locate behavioral patterns.
*   **The Strategic Decision:** By profiling exactly when, how, and why target segments make purchase choices, we establish clear bounds for regional distribution pipelines, eliminating dead inventory positions before product roll-out.
